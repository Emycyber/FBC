Our Story

SureCodes24 was founded with one simple mission — to make winning easier for everyday bettors in Nigeria and across Africa. We find out that booking your own games can be very time consuming and we decided to solve that problem.

We built SureCodes24 to solve that problem. All you have to do is come on our platform and copy the code of your preferred bookie and play or you may want to edit a little before staking.
